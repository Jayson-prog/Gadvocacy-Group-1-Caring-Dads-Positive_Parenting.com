DIY Project with Dad

Build a simple wooden toy or birdhouse together. Great bonding time with practical learning.

Materials:
  • Wood blocks
  • Hammer & nails
  • Paint (optional)
Steps:
  1. Choose a design with your child.
  2. Measure, cut, and assemble the parts.
  3. Decorate it together!

Weekend Sports Challenge

Go outside and play a sport your child enjoys! It builds trust and fitness together.

  • Basketball, soccer, or simple backyard catch
  • Compete in a friendly challenge or team up!
Story Time

Reading stories or sharing your own experiences is a great way to connect with your child.

  • Pick a storybook or tell them about your own childhood.
  • Ask them questions to keep it interactive!
Cook a Meal Together

Choose a simple recipe and prepare it with your child. Let them mix, cut (safely), or plate the food.

  • Pancakes, sandwiches, or fruit salad
  • Make it fun, and share responsibilities
